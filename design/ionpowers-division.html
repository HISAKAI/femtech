Ion Power S Wiping Cloth

High power cleaning wiping cloth of the doulbe powers of Alkaline electrolysis and surfactant.

USBC APPROVED ANYTIME (*USBC: United States Bowling Congress)

Ion Power S Wiping Cloth Effects

  • Effect on degreasing oil with the alcoholic solvent.
  • Effect on emulsifying and decomposing an oil spot with the nonionic surfactant.
  • Effect on removing a micro dirt with the non woven fabric of microscopic mesh.

Ion Power S Wiping Cloth How to Uses

  • Take out a sheet of cleaning cloth and close the bag with chuck tightly with releasing air.
  • Wipe a ball with a cloth widely spread on a palm of a hand.
  • The liquid is contained in a cloth enough to clean 3 balls or more.
  • After 30 seconds or 1 minute, the surface of a ball gets dry and non oily.

Ion Power S Wiping Cloth Precautions for use

  • Confirm the bag with chuck is closed tightly due to avoid vaporizing the alcoholic component.
  • Do not use for any purpose other than cleaning a ball.
  • Please Keep in a place out of the reach of children and away from direct sunlight.
  • In case of itching and irritate, please stop using it.
  • After cleaning a bowling ball, please wash hands immediately.

Ion Power S Wiping Cloth Specs

Ingredients: Alcoholic solvent, surfactant, alkali functional water and aroma chemical.

Size: 200mm*200mm.

Material: Rayon and polyester.
